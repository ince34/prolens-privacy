4. Photo Processing & Privacy

Photo enhancement is performed entirely on your device using our local processing engine. Your original photos are never uploaded to our servers.

For AI scene detection and parameter analysis, a resized version (max 1280px) of your photo may be temporarily transmitted to Google Gemini AI via Firebase Cloud Functions over an encrypted HTTPS connection.

We do not intentionally store uploaded photo data in our own databases or cloud storage. The resized image is transmitted only for the purpose of generating enhancement parameters and is not retained by ProLens AI after the request is completed. Processing by Google is subject to Google's applicable data processing and privacy terms.

We do not use your photos for AI model training or any purpose other than generating enhancement parameters for your session.

5. Automated Processing

ProLens AI uses automated image analysis to recommend photo adjustment parameters. This processing does not make decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects on users.

6. Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to show rewarded advertisements. For users in regions where consent or privacy choices are required, we use Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request and manage applicable advertising privacy choices before requesting ads.

Where required, a privacy options entry point will be made available in the app so that users can review applicable advertising privacy choices.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.
