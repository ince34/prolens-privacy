Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

ProLens AI is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Email address, authentication credentials (via Firebase Authentication), and registration date.
  • Usage statistics: Credits used, plan type, number of photos processed, and feature usage counts. These are retained for up to 12 months to manage your account and improve the service.
  • Device information: Android device ID (Android Advertising ID or hardware identifier), used solely for fraud prevention — specifically to enforce our one free credit per device policy. This ID is stored in our database linked to your account and is deleted when you delete your account.
  • Photos: See Section 3 below for full details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your identity and manage your account.
  • To manage your credit balance and subscription plan.
  • To provide AI-powered photo enhancement and analysis.
  • To prevent fraud and abuse of our credit system.
  • To improve app performance and user experience.

3. Photo Processing & Privacy

Photo enhancement (color correction, brightness, contrast adjustments) is performed entirely on your device using our local processing engine. Your original photos are never uploaded to our servers.

For AI scene detection and parameter analysis, a resized version (maximum 1280px) of the selected photo is securely transmitted to Google Gemini API through Firebase Cloud Functions over an encrypted HTTPS connection.

Photos are not permanently stored on our servers and are deleted after analysis is complete. We do not use your photos for training AI models or any purpose other than generating enhancement parameters for your session.

4. AI Generated Results Disclaimer

ProLens AI uses artificial intelligence to generate image enhancement recommendations and processing parameters. Results may vary depending on image quality, lighting conditions, and subject matter. Users remain responsible for reviewing processed images before commercial or professional use. ProLens AI makes no guarantee that processed images will meet specific quality standards or requirements.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and cloud functions. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini API: AI photo parameter analysis. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing: In-app purchases and subscriptions. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In: Optional authentication method.
  • RevenueCat (if applicable): Subscription management and purchase validation. RevenueCat Privacy Policy

6. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active.
  • Photos: Not stored after processing. Deleted immediately after analysis.
  • Usage statistics (photos processed count, credits used, plan type): Retained for up to 12 months.
  • Device ID: Retained until account deletion.
  • Upon account deletion: All personal data is removed within 30 days.

7. Your Rights (GDPR / KVKK)

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Data portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Object to processing of your personal data.

To exercise these rights, delete your account directly in the app or contact us at a.ince.mugla@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

ProLens AI is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

9. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including Firebase App Check (Play Integrity API), encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), and Firestore security rules that ensure users can only access their own data.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email. Continued use of ProLens AI after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

3. Fotoğraf İşleme ve Gizlilik

Fotoğraf iyileştirme (renk düzeltme, parlaklık, kontrast ayarları) tamamen cihazınızda yerel işleme motorumuz kullanılarak gerçekleştirilir. Orijinal fotoğraflarınız sunucularımıza hiçbir zaman yüklenmez.

AI sahne tespiti ve parametre analizi için seçilen fotoğrafın yeniden boyutlandırılmış versiyonu (maksimum 1280px), şifreli HTTPS bağlantısı üzerinden Firebase Cloud Functions aracılığıyla güvenli bir şekilde Google Gemini API'ye iletilir.

Fotoğraflar sunucularımızda kalıcı olarak saklanmaz ve analiz tamamlandıktan sonra silinir. Fotoğraflarınızı yapay zeka modellerini eğitmek veya iyileştirme parametreleri oluşturmak dışında herhangi bir amaçla kullanmıyoruz.
